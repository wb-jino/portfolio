Isaac Kise Lee

Isaac Kise Lee is a scientist, engineer, inventor, and entrepreneur. I am currently the CTO of NGENI foundation in San Diego California and Jiguwa Inc in Incheon S. Korea. I am interested in utilizing information from the human genome and applying computational skills to create novel services and products that will leverage the next wave of bio-based technological revolution. I was one of the first students in the US to have his whole genome sequenced and analyzed, and as a result, I became a strong advocate for utilizing valuable information contained in the genome to provide insight into individuals' biological blueprint. I conducted my undergraduate education at the University of California, Irvine (UCI) with a B.S. in Biological Sciences, and currently undergoing a master's program at the computer science department at Incheon National University (INU).
